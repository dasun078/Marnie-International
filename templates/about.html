About Us

Welcome to Marnie Internationals, where excellence meets opportunity. Our commitment to accuracy, honesty, and integrity stands as a beacon of trust in the dynamic world of recruitment.

Whether you are a candidate seeking your next career milestone or a skilled professional looking for guidance, our dedicated, transparent, and friendly team is here to assist you every step of the way.

Our Vision

To be the foremost catalyst in shaping dynamic career journeys, connecting talent with opportunity, and fostering a world where individuals thrive in roles that ignite their passion and potential.

Our Mission

Moving forward, ensuring mutual success and long-lasting reliability through integrity and personalized service, creating the most appropriate and tailored opportunities for experienced professionals pursuing their career dreams beyond Sri Lankan borders.

Who We Are

Marnie Internationals is a recruitment agency dedicated to aligning individuals with their skills, aspirations, and values. With over 1200 successful placements, we have helped many find success and have made significant contributions to Turkey's construction industry.

Our history began with the IGA project, and we have since been involved in several landmark projects including Bodrum, Istanbul, Kastamonu, and the Akkuyu Nuclear Power Plant, working with esteemed organizations such as IGA, Kalyon, Kolin, Akyapi, Ciftay, and Cengiz.
